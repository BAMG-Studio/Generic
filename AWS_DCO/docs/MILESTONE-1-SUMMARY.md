### Design Philosophy
**Assessment**: Flat hierarchy, purpose-driven
- **Evidence**: All 5 OUs at same level under Root
- **Alternative not chosen**: Nested hierarchy (e.g., by region or business unit)
- **Rationale**: Simplicity, clarity, scalability to 100+ accounts

### Naming Strategy
**Assessment**: Self-documenting, clear purpose
- **Evidence**: 
  - "Production-OU" clearly indicates production workloads
  - "Non-production-OU" clearly indicates dev/test
  - "Security-OU" clearly indicates security tooling
  - "Sandbox-OU" clearly indicates experimentation
  - "DO-NOT-TOUCH" in all-caps signals critical infrastructure
- **Best Practice**: Naming tells you what the OU is for without needing documentation

### Future SCP Strategy (Predictable from Structure)
**Inference**: Different SCPs for different OUs
- **Production-OU**: Strictest SCPs (change management, encryption required)
- **Non-production-OU**: Moderate SCPs (baseline security, some flexibility)
- **Sandbox-OU**: Minimal SCPs (budget limits, learning freedom)
- **Security-OU**: Prevent security tool disablement
- **DO-NOT-TOUCH**: Most restrictive (emergency access only)
